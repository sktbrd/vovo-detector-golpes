## Psychology Reference

### Frameworks Used:

1. **Loss Aversion:** "Não Perca R$5.000"
   - Losses feel 2x worse than equivalent gains
   - More effective than "Ganhe segurança"

2. **Anchoring:** "R$5.000", "847 Vítimas"
   - First number sets expectations
   - Makes threat feel real and specific

3. **Social Proof:** "12.540 pessoas", "847 vítimas"
   - People follow what others do
   - Popularity signals importance

4. **Scarcity/Urgency:** "Esta Semana", "AGORA", "Hoje"
   - Limited time creates pressure
   - Triggers fear of missing out (FOMO)

5. **Speed Promise:** "10 Segundos", "5 Minutos"
   - Immediate gratification
   - Lowers perceived effort
